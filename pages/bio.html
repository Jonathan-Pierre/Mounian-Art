Bio

About Wendyyy

Wendyyy — whose real name is Wendy Duvert and also known as Wendyyy Traka — is one of the most influential voices in Haitian rap and Creole hip‑hop. Born in Léogâne on March 23, 1989, he became a naturalized French citizen and moved to Guadeloupe in adolescence before returning to Haiti to build his career

In Summary

Wendyyy Traka has evolved from a local Guadeloupe-educated poet to the kingpin of Haitian rap. Renowned for his lyrical depth, strategic comebacks, and fervent fan community, he stands out as a creative force shaping Haitian culture. Whether it's breaking streaming records or fusing global and Creole styles, Wendyyy remains an artist to watch—both in Haiti and on the world stage.
